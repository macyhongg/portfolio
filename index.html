ABOUT ME

I am a full-time English teacher looking to make a career switch into Web Development. My interest began in 2019 when all schools moved to online learning and subsequently a hybrid model. I found myself being very intentional with how I structured the landing page and resources for my students, and assisted other educators in doing the same. It was fun to thnk about how the user would interact with the site, but frustrating that I had to work within the limits of what the LMS or hosting site could offer.

When I am not teaching or coding I love to read, snowboard, play engine-building board games (Wingspan!), or travel. I also have a dog and a cat, and an unhealthy addiction to milk tea.
